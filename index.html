PRIVACY POLICY

1. The Short Version

The ETLab Auto Assistant extension respects your privacy. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your personal data on external servers. Everything stays entirely on your local device.

2. Data Collection & Usage

This extension requires specific permissions to function, but it uses them strictly locally:

  • Host Permissions (*://*.etlab.in/*): The extension only activates and injects its automation scripts when you are actively browsing an ETLab website. It cannot read or modify data on any other websites you visit.
  • Local Storage: We use your browser's local memory simply to save the "Total Questions Answered" counter. This data is saved on your computer's hard drive and is never sent over the internet.

3. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, trackers, or any third-party background scripts. Your usage of the extension is completely private to you.

4. Developer & Contact
